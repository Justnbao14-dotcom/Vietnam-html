About Vietnam

Vietnam is a country located in Southeast Asia, bordering China, Laos, and Cambodia. It has a long coastline along the East Sea and a population of over 100 million people.

Vietnam is known for its strong national identity, shaped by thousands of years of history, foreign influences, and resilience. Today, it is a fast-growing country that still preserves its traditional values.

Vietnamese Culture

Vietnamese culture is influenced by Confucianism, Buddhism, and Taoism. Respect for elders, family bonds, and community harmony are deeply valued.

Key Cultural Values

  • Respect for family and ancestors
  • Hospitality toward guests
  • Strong sense of community
  • Hard work and perseverance

Traditional festivals such as Tet (Lunar New Year) play an important role in Vietnamese life and bring families together.

Vietnamese Cuisine

Vietnamese food is famous around the world for its balance of flavors, fresh ingredients, and healthy cooking methods.

Popular Vietnamese Dishes

  1. Pho – Noodle soup with beef or chicken
  2. Banh Mi – Vietnamese baguette sandwich
  3. Spring Rolls – Fresh or fried rolls
  4. Bun Cha – Grilled pork with noodles
